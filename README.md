# College Attendance & Performance Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between student attendance and academic performance using **Python** and **Power BI**.
The goal is to identify patterns between attendance and marks, analyze subject-wise performance, and detect students who are at risk of failing.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze the correlation between **attendance and academic performance**
* Identify **subject-wise performance trends**
* Visualize **pass vs fail distribution**
* Detect **students at risk of failing** based on attendance and internal marks

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Microsoft Power BI

## 📈 Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes the following visualizations:

1. **Attendance vs Final Marks (Scatter Plot)**
   Shows the relationship between attendance and academic performance.

2. **Pass vs Fail Distribution (Pie Chart)**
   Displays the proportion of students who passed or failed.

3. **Average Marks by Subject (Bar Chart)**
   Highlights subject-wise student performance.

4. **Students at Risk Table**
   Identifies students with **low attendance and low internal marks** who may fail.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Students with **attendance above 80%** tend to score higher marks.
* Students with **attendance below 50%** are more likely to fail.
* Certain subjects show **lower average marks**, indicating higher difficulty.
* Early identification of **at-risk students** can help improve academic support.
